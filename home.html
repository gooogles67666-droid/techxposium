ABOUT SCE

Supaul College of Engineering, located in Bihar, established in 2017, is a public institute approved by AICTE. It provides 7 UG and PG courses in 6 specialisations including Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering

ABOUT TECHXPOSIUM

TechXposium is the flagship technical fest of SCE Supaul, organized by the Technical Club under the guidance of faculty advisors. The fest brings together students, professionals and innovators to celebrate technology, learning and collaboration. competitions and creativity together for 3 exciting days.
